Our Story

"Every saree carries a story — of the hands that wove it, the traditions that inspired it, and the woman who wears it."

Sorayah was born out of a deep reverence for India's textile heritage. Founded in 2007 in the heart of Lucknow, we began with a simple mission: to connect the extraordinary craftsmanship of Indian weavers with women across the country who appreciate true artistry.


Over 18 years, we have built lasting relationships with over 500 weaver families across Varanasi, Kanchipuram, Surat, and Pochampally. Each saree in our collection is personally curated by our design team and quality-checked to meet our luxury standards.


We believe luxury should not come at the cost of authenticity. When you choose Sorayah, you are not just buying a saree — you are preserving a centuries-old tradition and supporting the livelihoods of master artisans.

Return & Refund Policy

Return Window

We offer a 7-day return policy from the date of delivery. Items must be unused, unwashed, and in their original packaging with all tags intact. A package-opening(unboxing) video is mandatory for all return requests. Return claims without an unboxing video will not be accepted.

How to Initiate a Return

WhatsApp us at +91 9519456330 or email Supportsorayahdrapes@gmail.com with your order number and photos of the item. Our team will arrange pickup within 48 hours.

Refund Timeline

Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days after we receive and inspect the returned item. For COD orders, refunds are made via bank transfer or UPI.

Non-Returnable Items

Custom-stitched blouses, sale items, and items damaged due to misuse are not eligible for return. Shipping charges are non-refundable.

Exchange

We happily offer exchanges for different sizes or designs subject to availability. Exchange shipping is free for your first request.

Your Data, Our Responsibility

Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

We collect your name, email, phone number, and delivery address when you place an order. We may also collect browsing data to improve your experience on our website.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to process your orders, provide customer support, and send you updates about your order. We do not sell your data to third parties.

WhatsApp Communication

By providing your phone number, you consent to receiving order updates and promotional messages on WhatsApp. You may opt out at any time.

Data Security

Fast & Reliable

Shipping Policy

Processing Time

Orders are processed within 1–2 business days. Custom orders may take 5–7 additional days for preparation.

Delivery Timeline

Standard delivery takes 4–7 business days across India. Express delivery (2–3 days) is available in major cities at an additional charge.

Shipping Charges

Free shipping on orders above ₹2,999. Orders below ₹2,999 incur a flat shipping charge of ₹99. Express shipping is ₹199 extra.

Cash on Delivery

COD is available pan India. A COD handling fee of ₹49 applies. COD is not available for orders above ₹15,000.

Tracking

You will receive an SMS and WhatsApp message with your tracking number once your order is dispatched. Use our Track Order page for live updates.

International Shipping

We currently ship to UAE, USA, UK, Canada, and Australia. International shipping takes 10–15 business days. Customs duties are borne by the customer.
